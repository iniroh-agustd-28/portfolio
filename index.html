About Me

I'm Rohini, a UI/UX designer with a passion for creating meaningful digital experiences that make a difference. With over 5 years of experience, I specialize in user research, interface design, and design systems.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring new design trends, sketching ideas, or enjoying a good cup of coffee.

My Process

01

Understanding the problem, researching users, and defining project goals through interviews and analysis.

02

Creating user personas, mapping journeys, and establishing clear design requirements and constraints.

03

Design

Sketching ideas, creating wireframes, and developing high-fidelity designs with attention to detail.

Building interactive prototypes to test functionality and gather feedback before development.

05

Test

Conducting usability tests, gathering insights, and iterating based on user feedback.

06

Deliver
